About Us

Your trusted partner for unforgettable Sri Lankan adventures

10+ Years Experience

Trusted Local Business in Mirissa

Located in the heart of Kaluwella Watta, Mirissa, GUNASEKARA TRANSPORT SERVICE has been serving locals and tourists with dedication and passion. We specialize in providing comprehensive transport and tourism solutions that showcase the best of Sri Lanka.

Led by owner M.H.T.R. Gunasekara, our team of experienced drivers and guides ensures every journey is safe, comfortable, and memorable.
